## What to Look For in an Email Marketing Service

Before comparing platforms, know what matters:

- **Deliverability** — does it actually reach the inbox?
- **Automation** — can you build triggered sequences?
- **Segmentation** — can you target specific audience segments?
- **Templates** — quality of pre-built designs
- **Analytics** — open rates, clicks, revenue attribution
- **Integrations** — connects with your CRM, e-commerce, website
- **Pricing model** — per subscriber, per email, or flat rate
- **Ease of use** — can you launch a campaign without a developer?

## Top Email Marketing Services Compared

### 1. Mailchimp — Best for Beginners

**What it is:** The most well-known email marketing platform. Easy to use, generous free tier, and broad feature set.

**Key features:**
- Drag-and-drop email builder
- Pre-built templates (100+)
- Basic automation (welcome series, abandoned cart)
- Audience segmentation
- Landing pages and forms
- A/B testing
- Reporting and analytics
- 300+ integrations

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Contacts | Sends/Month |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | 500 | 1,000 |
| Essentials | $13/mo | 500 | 5,000 |
| Standard | $20/mo | 500 | 6,000 |
| Premium | $350/mo | 10,000 | 150,000 |

*Prices increase with contact count.*

**Best for:** Small businesses, solopreneurs, and beginners who want simplicity.
**Limitation:** Automation is basic compared to dedicated platforms. Gets expensive as your list grows.

### 2. ConvertKit (Kit) — Best for Creators

**What it is:** Email marketing built for creators — bloggers, podcasters, YouTubers, course creators.

**Key features:**
- Visual automation builder
- Tag-based subscriber management (no lists)
- Landing pages and opt-in forms
- Creator-focused templates
- Digital product sales (built-in commerce)
- Newsletter referral system
- Sponsor network for monetization

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Subscribers |
|------|-------|------------|
| Newsletter (Free) | $0 | 10,000 |
| Creator | $29/mo | 1,000 |
| Creator Pro | $59/mo | 1,000 |

*Scales with subscriber count.*

**Best for:** Content creators, newsletter writers, course sellers.
**Limitation:** Limited design flexibility. Not ideal for e-commerce or complex marketing.

### 3. ActiveCampaign — Best for Automation

**What it is:** Advanced marketing automation platform with email, CRM, and sales tools combined.

**Key features:**
- Advanced automation builder (if/then logic, scoring, split paths)
- Built-in CRM with pipeline management
- Site tracking and event-based triggers
- Predictive sending (AI-optimized send times)
- Deep segmentation
- SMS marketing
- 900+ integrations

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Lite | $29/mo | Email + basic automation |
| Plus | $49/mo | CRM + advanced automation |
| Professional | $149/mo | Predictive sending + split automation |
| Enterprise | $259/mo | Custom everything |

*Based on 1,000 contacts. Scales with list size.*

**Best for:** B2B companies and e-commerce brands that need sophisticated automation.
**Limitation:** Steeper learning curve. Overkill for simple newsletters.

### 4. Klaviyo — Best for E-Commerce

**What it is:** Email and SMS marketing purpose-built for e-commerce stores.

**Key features:**
- Deep Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce integration
- Pre-built e-commerce flows (abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back)
- Product recommendations in emails
- Revenue attribution per email/flow
- Advanced segmentation based on purchase behavior
- SMS marketing included
- Predictive analytics (customer lifetime value, churn risk)

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Contacts | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| Free | 250 | $0 |
| Email | 1,000 | $30/mo |
| Email + SMS | 1,000 | $45/mo |

*Scales significantly with contact count. 50K contacts = ~$700/mo.*

**Best for:** E-commerce stores (especially Shopify).
**Limitation:** Expensive at scale. Not ideal for non-e-commerce.

### 5. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — Best Value

**Key features:**
- Email marketing with drag-and-drop builder
- SMS and WhatsApp campaigns
- Marketing automation
- CRM included (free)
- Transactional email
- Landing pages
- Pricing based on emails sent (not contacts)

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Emails/Month | Contacts |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 300/day | Unlimited |
| Starter | $25/mo | 20,000 | Unlimited |
| Business | $65/mo | 20,000 | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

**Best for:** Budget-conscious businesses who want unlimited contacts.
**Limitation:** Deliverability has mixed reviews. Interface less polished than competitors.

## FAQ

### Which email marketing service has the best deliverability?
ActiveCampaign and ConvertKit consistently rank highest for deliverability. Mailchimp is middle-of-the-road. Brevo varies. Deliverability depends partly on your own sending practices.

### Can I switch email marketing platforms easily?
Yes — most platforms allow CSV import of subscriber lists. You'll lose automation history and some segmentation data, but contacts transfer easily. Export before canceling.

### How many subscribers do I need before paying?
Most platforms have useful free tiers up to 500-2,500 subscribers. Start free, upgrade when you need automation, better templates, or hit the contact limit.

### Is email marketing still worth it in 2026?
Absolutely. Email generates $36 per $1 spent — the highest ROI of any marketing channel. 4+ billion people use email. Social algorithms change; your email list is yours forever.
