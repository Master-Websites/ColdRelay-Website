## Cost Components Most People Forget

Before we dive into the numbers, let's catalog every cost involved in running cold email infrastructure. Most comparisons only cover mailbox costs — here's what they miss:

### 1. Mailbox Licensing
The base cost per email account. This is what most people compare, but it's only 40-60% of the total.

### 2. Domain Costs
Every mailbox needs a domain. How many mailboxes you can run per domain varies dramatically by provider:
- Google Workspace: **2 per domain**
- Microsoft 365: **~5 per domain**
- ColdRelay: **up to 150 per domain**

Domain cost: ~$10-15/year each (we'll use $12/year average).

### 3. Warmup Tools
New mailboxes need warmup to build sending reputation. Some providers include warmup; others require third-party tools:
- **Included:** ColdRelay, Instantly, Smartlead
- **Not included:** Google Workspace, Microsoft 365
- Third-party warmup cost: ~$3-5/mailbox/month (Warmbox, Lemwarm)

### 4. Email Verification
You need to verify every email address before sending. Bad lists destroy deliverability:
- Cost: ~$0.003-0.005 per verification
- At scale: 50,000 verifications/month = $150-250/month
- This cost is the same regardless of infrastructure

### 5. Sending Tool
Your sending/sequencing tool (Instantly, Smartlead, Woodpecker, etc.) is a separate cost. Since this is the same regardless of infrastructure, we'll note it but exclude it from the comparison.

### 6. DNS Management Time
Every domain needs DNS records configured (SPF, DKIM, DMARC). This takes time:
- Manual (Google/Microsoft): ~30-45 minutes per domain
- Automated (ColdRelay): ~0 minutes

We'll value setup time at $50/hour (cost of a technical ops person or your own time).

### 7. Ongoing Management
Domain renewals, mailbox monitoring, rotation of aged mailboxes, DNS updates, troubleshooting suspensions. This scales with domain count.

### 8. Deliverability Monitoring
Understanding where your emails land:
- **Included:** ColdRelay
- **Not included:** Google Workspace, Microsoft 365
- Third-party tools (GlockApps, etc.): ~$90-200/month

### 9. Blacklist Monitoring
Checking if your IPs/domains are on blacklists:
- **Included:** ColdRelay
- **Not included:** Google/Microsoft
- Third-party tools: ~$30-80/month

## The Hidden Costs Nobody Talks About

### Account Suspensions

Google and Microsoft regularly suspend accounts used for cold email. Each suspension means:
- **Lost sending capacity** while you set up replacements
- **Wasted warmup time** (2-4 weeks per new mailbox)
- **New domain purchases** if the domain is flagged
- **Lost momentum** on active campaigns

At 500 mailboxes on Google, expect **5-10% suspension rate per month**. That's 25-50 mailboxes needing replacement constantly. The recurring cost of suspension recovery:

```
50 mailboxes × ($12 domain + $7.20 setup + 3 weeks warmup lost productivity)
= ~$2,000/month in suspension-related costs
```

With dedicated infrastructure designed for outbound, suspension rates drop to near zero.

### Opportunity Cost

Every hour spent managing infrastructure is an hour not spent on campaigns, targeting, or closing deals.

At 500 mailboxes:
- Google Workspace management: **~20 hours/month**
- ColdRelay management: **~1 hour/month**

That's **19 hours/month** of reclaimed productivity — nearly **half a work week** every month.

### Domain Reputation Risk

With Google Workspace, you're cycling through hundreds of domains. Each domain has a limited lifespan for cold email before reputation degrades. You're constantly:
- Buying new domains
- Aging them (ideally 2+ weeks before use)
- Warming up mailboxes on new domains
- Retiring burned domains

This "domain treadmill" is an ongoing hidden cost that's hard to quantify but very real.

With ColdRelay's 150-mailbox-per-domain ratio, your domains last much longer because the infrastructure isolation protects domain reputation.
