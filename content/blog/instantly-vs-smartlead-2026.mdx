# Instantly vs Smartlead in 2026: The Honest Comparison

Instantly and Smartlead are the two most popular cold email platforms in 2026. Both offer unlimited email accounts, email warmup, and multi-step sequences. Both have grown significantly. And both have significant limitations nobody talks about.

We've used both extensively. Here's the real comparison — no affiliate bias.

## Quick Verdict

| Aspect | Winner | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Features** | Instantly | 100+ API endpoints, lead database, email verification |
| **Pricing (at scale)** | Smartlead | $379 for 500K vs Instantly's $358+ (but Instantly gates features) |
| **API & Automation** | Instantly | 100 req/sec vs Smartlead's 5 req/2sec |
| **Deliverability** | Tie | Both use shared infrastructure |
| **Agency Features** | Smartlead | Better client-level isolation |
| **Simplicity** | Smartlead | Less feature bloat, cleaner UI |
| **Overall** | Depends | Instantly for features, Smartlead for agencies |

**Neither wins on infrastructure.** Both use shared IPs. For best deliverability, use dedicated infrastructure (like ColdRelay) underneath either tool.

### The Hidden Pricing Problem with Instantly

Instantly's base plan ($37) looks cheap but it's limited:
- 5,000 emails/month (not enough for serious outbound)
- No A/B testing (requires $97+ plan)
- No webhooks (requires $97+ plan)
- No advanced analytics (requires $97+ plan)

And if you want the full ecosystem:
- Sending: $97/mo (Hypergrowth)
- Leads: $97/mo (Growth Leads)
- CRM: $47/mo

**Total for full Instantly stack: $241/mo minimum.**

Smartlead's $94/mo Pro plan includes everything — A/B testing, webhooks, analytics, 150K emails. More straightforward.
